Embroidery Designer (f/m/d)

Minimum 2 days per week Lustenau, Vorarlberg Remote work possible

Hämmerle & Vogel — a family business in Lustenau, Vorarlberg. For over 60 years, we've been designing and producing embroideries for international fashion and lingerie brands — Designed in Austria — produced worldwide. Our clients: Victoria's Secret, Marks & Spencer, Agent Provocateur, Akris, and many more. What our team creates here, people wear around the world.

With Lara Hämmerle, the third generation is now taking over — someone who previously built tech companies and brings the drive to evolve the business: new approaches to design, new technologies, new ways of working with clients. Built on 60 years of embroidery expertise and a team that knows its craft. If you love creating beautiful things and want to explore new ground while doing it, you're in the right place.

What you'll do
  • You design embroideries for international brands — from the initial idea to the finished refined sketch.
  • You translate trends, themes, and moods into embroiderable patterns.
  • You immerse yourself in a brand's DNA, interpret client briefings, and independently develop designs from them.
  • You work on seasonal collections and customer-specific development — and implement changes quickly and precisely.
  • You collaborate closely with an experienced design team and work in direct exchange with clients.
What you bring
  • You have a distinctive creative signature and create new sketches from scratch.
  • You bring experience in textile design — ideally in embroidery or lingerie — and have an understanding of embroidery technique.
  • You work independently, bring creative courage, and hold yourself to the standard of delivering the best design possible.
  • You communicate openly and enjoy working in a team and with clients.
  • You're curious about new tools, techniques, and technologies and want to constantly develop further.
What we offer
  • A small, experienced design team with decades of embroidery expertise.
  • Work with international brands, from lingerie to haute couture.
  • Space for creative independence and bold design thinking.
  • Direct collaboration with leadership and short paths in a company that's moving forward with new energy.
  • A vertical company, from design to finished product — embroidery up close.
  • Annual professional development budget.
  • Remote work possible.

Scope: Minimum 2 days per week — with the option for more.
