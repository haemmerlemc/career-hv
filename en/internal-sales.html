Internal Sales Manager (f/m/d)

Full-time or 80% Lustenau, Vorarlberg Remote days possible

Hämmerle & Vogel — a family business in Lustenau, Vorarlberg. For over 60 years, we've been designing and producing embroideries for international fashion and lingerie brands — Designed in Austria — produced worldwide. Our clients: Victoria's Secret, Marks & Spencer, Agent Provocateur, Akris, and many more. What our team creates here, people wear around the world.

With Lara Hämmerle, the third generation is now taking over — someone who previously built tech companies and brings the drive to evolve the business: new approaches to design, new technologies, new ways of working with clients. Built on 60 years of embroidery expertise and a team that knows its craft. If you're ambitious and want to make sure great designs become successful orders, you're in the right place.

What you'll do
  • You own the commercial process from the moment a design sketch is approved — through to the order being produced and delivered to the client.
  • You get our samples to clients — coordinating production with the design team, gathering feedback, and making sure we're faster than the competition.
  • You prepare pricing requests, calculate costs, and ensure we offer competitively while protecting our margins.
  • You guide clients through the decision process — and turn samples into orders.
  • You manage our production partners, monitor delivery timelines, and ensure everything runs on schedule from order entry to shipment.
  • You're the operational link between clients, design, production, and logistics — our sales team sets the strategic direction, you make sure the engine runs.
What you bring
  • You have experience in a client-facing role — account management, vendor management, customer service, or similar.
  • You communicate clearly and confidently — with clients, production partners, and the team, in German and English — and you actively bring the client perspective into the company.
  • You spot improvement potential, develop solutions independently, and implement them.
  • You understand that speed and service excellence are why clients stay with us — and you have the eye for detail to deliver both.
  • You want to learn and grow — new technologies, new processes, and new challenges are what motivate you to keep developing.
What we offer
  • Work with international premium brands, from lingerie to haute couture.
  • A vertical company, from design to finished product.
  • Collaboration with an experienced team and short decision paths in a company that's changing fast.
  • Annual professional development budget.
  • Remote work possible.

Interested? Get in touch — we look forward to your application and an initial conversation.
