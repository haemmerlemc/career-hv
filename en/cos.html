Transformation

Chief of Staff / Transformation Lead (f/m/d)

30-40h/week Lustenau, Vorarlberg Remote days possible

Hämmerle & Vogel — a family business in Lustenau, Vorarlberg. For over 60 years, we've been designing and producing embroideries for international fashion and lingerie brands. Our clients: Victoria's Secret, Marks & Spencer, Akris, Agent Provocateur and many more.

The third generation is bringing a fresh start: Lara Hämmerle — who previously built tech companies — is leading the company into a new phase. The core stays: a product worn around the world. But the way we work is changing — with AI-powered tools, new processes, and a clear vision for how a 60-year-old company operates today. For that, we're looking for someone to build this transformation with us. Not as a consultant. As a builder.

What you'll do
  • Build internal tools and automations using AI — from a searchable design archive to CRM, from pipeline tracking to BI dashboards. Not classical software engineering, but prototyping with Claude, Cursor, Lovable, or whatever gets to results fastest.
  • Write grant applications and coordinate the funding of our transformation — FFG, aws, WISTO, IFB. Over €150K in available grants are waiting to be applied for.
  • Keep the threads together: What's the status of our strategic pillars? What's stuck? What needs a decision from the CEO? You prep meetings, track action items, and make sure plans turn into results.
What you bring
  • You've built something with AI tools — a prototype, an automation, a side project, no matter how small. You can show it.
  • You write clearly and structured, in German and English. Grant applications, internal documents, project briefs — anything that creates clarity.
  • You're self-directed. You get "here's the problem" and come back with "here's what I built / found / recommend."
  • You're comfortable with numbers — not as an accountant, but you can read a P&L, understand what margin means, and don't zone out when financials come up.
  • You're based in Vorarlberg or willing to be on-site in Lustenau 3-4 days per week. The team is small — presence matters.
Strong signals
  • You've built something with AI that actually works — a tool, a bot, an internal dashboard.
  • You've written a grant application, business plan, or strategy document before.
  • You come from a startup, agency, or consulting background where you wore multiple hats.
  • You find "60-year-old embroidery company goes AI-first" more interesting than "Series B SaaS."
What we offer
  • Direct collaboration with the CEO. No middle management, no long chains.
  • Immediate impact — there's a real backlog of things that need to be built and funded.
  • Full transparency: strategy, finances, decision-making — you're in the room when it counts.
  • A learning environment: AI tools, manufacturing, international B2B, family business transformation.
  • Annual professional development budget.
  • Flexibility on working hours and remote days.

Hours: 30-40h/week (20h/week minimum). The role works better full-time — but we'll find a solution.
